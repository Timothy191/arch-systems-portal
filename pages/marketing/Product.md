# Product Specifications: Marketing & Legal Module

## 1. Overview & Business Value
Houses public facing legal declarations, privacy policies, Terms of Use, and GDPR/POPIA compliant cookie consent configurations.

## 2. Target Personas
- **Public Visitors**: Access site terms, policies, and company statements.
- **Compliance Officers**: Audit terms to ensure strict alignment with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) and other regulations.

## 3. Core Features & Capabilities
- **Legal Document Viewer**: Standard layout rendering terms of service, privacy, and disclaimer policies.
- **Cookie Consent Banner**: Opt-in/opt-out choice toggles for functional, analytics, and marketing cookies.

## 4. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) & Metrics
- **Consent Rate**: Percentage of visitors who accept or configure cookie choices.
- **Policy Read Ratio**: Traffic to legal pages compared to main site traffic.

## 5. Security & Access Boundaries
Publicly accessible pages with no auth required. Writes to cookie preferences are stored on the client side only.
