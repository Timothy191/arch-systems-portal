# Design Specifications: Marketing & Legal Module

## 1. Design System & Aesthetics
A clean, readable typography-focused layout. Avoids heavy imagery or complex dashboards to focus entirely on reading comfort and legal clarity.

## 2. Key UI Layouts & Components
- **Document View**: A two-column structure with document sections on the left and reading text on the right.
- **Cookie Settings Modal**: Centered modal overlay listing cookie types and description switches.

## 3. Micro-Animations & Interactions
- **Smooth Scroll**: Clicking section headers in the sidebar scrolls the main text block smoothly into place.
- **Cookie Choice Slide**: Banners slide up from the viewport bottom when no consent is recorded.

## 4. Typography & Color Palette (Semantic Tokens)
Uses `Outfit` for document titles and serif-like highly readable typography for paragraphs. Colors: Clean greys, white text, and minimal green indicators.

## 5. Responsive Behavior & Breakpoints
Standard text layouts wrapping fluidly. The sidebar navigation converts to a top dropdown on mobile viewports.
