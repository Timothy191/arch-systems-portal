# Product Specifications: Safety Module

## 1. Overview & Business Value
Ensures site-wide regulatory compliance, tracks incident logs, conducts safety audits, and displays safety achievements like LTI-free days.

## 2. Target Personas
- **Safety Officers (HSE)**: Log incidents, track inspection status, and issue compliance notices.
- **All Personnel**: Access safety logs, report hazards, and verify safety status.
- **Auditors**: Access historical safety reports to ensure POPIA and mining safety act compliance.

## 3. Core Features & Capabilities
- **Incident Logging**: Formal portal to log site incidents, hazards, and near-misses.
- **Audit Checklist**: Interactive tools to conduct monthly safety audits and inspections.
- **Safety Milestones**: Visual tracking of safety metrics such as LTI-free days and training completion.

## 4. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) & Metrics
- **LTI-Free Days**: Consecutive days operating without a Lost Time Injury.
- **Incident Close-Out Rate**: Percentage of reported hazards resolved within target timeframes.
- **Audits Completed**: Percentage of scheduled safety audits executed.

## 5. Security & Access Boundaries
Incident submissions can be done by all logged-in employees, but editing or resolving logs is restricted to the safety team and administrators.
