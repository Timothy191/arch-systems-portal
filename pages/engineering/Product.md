# Product Specifications: Engineering Module

## 1. Overview & Business Value
Manages heavy machinery specifications, maintenance scheduling, active breakdowns, and tire wear telemetry to maximize equipment availability.

## 2. Target Personas
- **Maintenance Engineers**: Need detailed logs of mechanical breakdowns and history to plan repairs.
- **Tire Specialists**: Require telemetry data on haul truck tires (pressure, temperature) to prevent blowouts.
- **Parts Managers**: Track tool and spare parts inventories associated with engineering tasks.

## 3. Core Features & Capabilities
- **Breakdown Log**: Real-time reporting and status tracking of broken-down machinery.
- **Tire Management**: Telemetry dashboard tracking tire temperature, pressure, and remaining tread depth.
- **Maintenance Schedules**: Calendar interface mapping out scheduled downtime and services.

## 4. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) & Metrics
- **Mean Time to Repair (MTTR)**: Average hours taken to resolve a machinery breakdown.
- **Active Breakdowns**: Count of machinery currently offline due to failures.
- **Tire Alert Rate**: Percentage of tires operating outside optimal pressure/temperature parameters.

## 5. Security & Access Boundaries
Accessible by Engineering, Maintenance, and Admin departments. RLS protects records from modification by non-engineering personnel.
