# Product Specifications: Training Module

## 1. Overview & Business Value
Manages employee training programs, tracks mandatory operator certifications, lists schedules, and hosts learning modules to guarantee a qualified workforce.

## 2. Target Personas
- **Training Coordinators**: Create courses, monitor pass rates, and schedule practical certifications.
- **Operators**: Need to view their certification status, register for upcoming training, and complete LMS modules.
- **HR Managers**: Audit employee credentials to ensure only qualified personnel are assigned to high-risk machinery.

## 3. Core Features & Capabilities
- **Course Catalog**: Directory of available safety, operational, and technical training courses.
- **Certification Tracker**: Personal profile views displaying active, expiring, and lapsed credentials.
- **Training Scheduler**: Calendar listing scheduled classroom sessions and practical assessments.

## 4. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) & Metrics
- **Certification Compliance**: Percentage of active operators holding up-to-date credentials.
- **Course Completion Rate**: Percentage of registered students who successfully finish training.
- **Expiry Alerts**: Number of certifications expiring within the next 30 days.

## 5. Security & Access Boundaries
Operators can read and register for courses. Modifying grades, certifications, and scheduling requires a Training department or Admin role.
